## Introduction

Welcome to the **EDB Agentforce Workshop**.

At the Economic Development Board (EDB), our officers' greatest asset is their judgement —
knowing which companies to court and how to make the case for Singapore. But that value is
often sidelined by the "Research Tax" — the hours spent piecing together scattered
intelligence about a company, summarizing it by hand, and drafting outreach from scratch.
Today, we are building a **Smart Account Research Assistant** — a digital ally that turns
raw company intel into a structured profile and drafts the outreach to move the deal
forward.

### What You Will Achieve

Across three hands-on exercises, you'll go from raw AI to a trusted digital coworker:

1. **The Building Blocks (Prompt Builder)** — Craft **reusable prompts** grounded in your
   real account records and the web, so the AI speaks from facts, not guesses. Research a
   company into a structured **Smart Account Profile**, then draft a personalized investor
   outreach email.
2. **The Coworker (Agentforce Coworker)** — Put AI to work as a **copilot** right beside
   your team. Through **Agentforce Coworker**, officers simply ask in plain language —
   *"Research Helios Robotics and tell me if they're a fit"* — and get grounded answers
   inline, no clicks through Setup required.
3. **The Doer (Agent Builder)** — Turn those prompts into an autonomous **agent that takes
   action**. Watch the reasoning engine decide *on its own* when to look up an account,
   assess investment fit, and enrich the profile.
