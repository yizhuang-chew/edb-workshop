## Introduction

Welcome to the **EDB Agentforce Workshop**.

At the Economic Development Board (EDB), too much officer time goes into the "Research
Tax" — piecing together scattered intel on a company and drafting outreach by hand.

Today, we'll build a **Smart Account Research Assistant** that turns raw company intel
into a structured profile and drafts the outreach to move the deal forward.

### What You Will Achieve

By the end of this workshop, you will be able to:

1. **Write AI prompts** that research a company into a **Smart Account Profile** and draft
   an investor outreach email. *(Prompt Builder)*
2. **Use an AI copilot** beside the search bar to help with everyday tasks. *(Agentforce
   Coworker)*
3. **Build an AI agent** that researches an account and assesses its fit on its own.
   *(Agent Builder)*
